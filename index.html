Policy Simulation

Choose your policies to reduce cost and CO2

Now take the role of a policymaker. You can change policies that affect utility planning. You can also change the cost assumptions for future energy sources. Now, the utility planning is automatic: the utility planner will always keep the lights on at the least cost. See how your policies and cost assumptions affect CO2 and costs to consumers.
